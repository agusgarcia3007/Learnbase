AI tools to help you create course content faster.

## Video analysis

When you upload a video, the AI can:

- **Transcribe**: Convert speech to text automatically
- **Generate title**: Suggest a title based on content
- **Generate description**: Write a summary of the lesson

Go to **Content > Videos**, select a video, and click **Analyze**.

## Quiz generation

Generate quiz questions from your content:

1. Go to **Content > Quizzes**
2. Open a quiz
3. Click **Generate Questions**
4. Select a video or document as source
5. Choose how many questions

The AI creates relevant questions with:

- Multiple choice options
- Correct answers marked
- Explanations for each answer

Review and edit before publishing.

## Course metadata

Generate course information from your modules:

1. Go to your course
2. Click **Generate with AI**

The AI analyzes all content and suggests:

- Course title
- Description
- Learning objectives
- Thumbnail image

## Theme generation

Get color scheme suggestions:

1. Go to **Site > Customization**
2. Click **Generate Theme**
3. Describe your brand (e.g., "professional, blue tones, modern")

The AI creates a complete color palette for light and dark modes.

## Tips

- AI suggestions are starting points, always review and adjust
- Better source content leads to better AI output
- Use clear audio in videos for accurate transcription
