## Basic information

Go to **Content > Courses** and click **New Course**.

Fill in the required fields:

- **Title**: The name of your course
- **Description**: What students will learn
- **Category**: How to classify it
- **Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced

## Media

Add visual elements:

- **Thumbnail**: The cover image that appears in the catalog
- **Preview video**: A short video that students can watch before enrolling

## Pricing

Configure the price:

- **Price**: The amount to charge
- **Currency**: USD, EUR, or your local currency
- **Original price**: If you want to show a discount

## Additional details

Optionally add:

- **Features**: What's included (e.g., "10 hours of video", "Certificate")
- **Requirements**: What students need to know beforehand
- **Objectives**: What they'll be able to do after completing the course

## Certificate

Enable the certificate option if you want students to receive a certificate when they complete the course.
