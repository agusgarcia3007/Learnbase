Make your campus match your brand identity.

## Logo and favicon

Go to **Site > Customization**:

- **Logo**: Appears in the header. Recommended: PNG with transparent background.
- **Favicon**: The small icon in browser tabs. Recommended: Square, at least 32x32px.

## Theme

Choose from preset themes:

- Default
- Slate
- Rose
- Emerald
- Tangerine
- Ocean

Each theme defines colors for buttons, links, and accents.

## Colors

For more control, customize individual colors:

- **Primary**: Main action color (buttons, links)
- **Background**: Page background
- **Text**: Main text color

Colors adapt automatically for light and dark mode.

## Typography

Choose your fonts:

- **Heading font**: For titles
- **Body font**: For regular text

## Background patterns

Add visual interest to your pages:

- Grid
- Dots
- Waves
- None

Patterns appear subtly behind content.

## Preview

Changes show in real-time as you adjust settings. Click **Save** when you're happy with the result.
