Help people find your campus through search engines.

## Meta tags

Go to **Site > Configuration** and set:

- **SEO Title**: Appears in browser tabs and search results
- **SEO Description**: The snippet shown in search results
- **Keywords**: Terms related to your content

## Hero section

Customize your landing page:

- **Hero Title**: Main headline
- **Hero Subtitle**: Supporting text
- **Call to Action**: Button text (e.g., "Start Learning")

## Social links

Add your social media profiles:

- Twitter/X
- Facebook
- Instagram
- LinkedIn
- YouTube

These appear in your campus footer and help with social proof.

## Footer

Customize the footer text that appears on all pages.

## Tips

- Keep your SEO title under 60 characters
- Write a description between 150-160 characters
- Use relevant keywords naturally
- Update your hero section to reflect current offerings
