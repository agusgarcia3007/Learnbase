Learnbase is a platform for creating and managing online courses. Each organization gets its own campus with custom branding, domain, and content.

## Who is it for?

- **Training companies** looking to launch their online academy
- **Educational institutions** that want to digitize their courses
- **Companies** that need to train their teams
- **Creators** who want to sell their courses
