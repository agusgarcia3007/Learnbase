After creating your campus, complete these steps to get started:

## 1. Add your brand

Go to **Site > Customization** and upload:

- Your logo (appears in the header)
- Your favicon (browser tab icon)
- Choose your theme colors

## 2. Create a category

Go to **Content > Categories** and create at least one category to organize your courses (e.g., "Marketing", "Sales", "Programming").

## 3. Add an instructor

Go to **Content > Instructors** and add the instructors who will teach your courses. Include:

- Name and photo
- Short bio
- Contact links

## 4. Create your first course

Go to **Content > Courses** and create your first course. You'll need:

- Title and description
- Thumbnail image
- At least one module with content

## 5. Invite your first student

Go to **Management > Users** and add a student to test your course.
