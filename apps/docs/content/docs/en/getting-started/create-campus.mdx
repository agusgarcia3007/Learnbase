## Sign up

1. Go to **learnbase.lat** and click **Get Started**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Check your inbox and click the verification link

## Create your campus

Once verified, you can create your campus:

1. Choose a unique name (this will be your subdomain: `yourname.learnbase.lat`)
2. Add a description of your organization
3. Click **Create**

Your campus is now ready. You'll see the onboarding checklist to help you set everything up.

## What's next?

- Upload your logo and customize your brand
- Create your first course
- Invite your first student
